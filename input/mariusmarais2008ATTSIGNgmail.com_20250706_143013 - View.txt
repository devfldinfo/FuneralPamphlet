\begin{hymn}{92}{God is longing}{}
\begin{stanza}
God is longing now to make you\\
Like unto His Holy One,\\
That you may reflect His glory\\
While this life's short race you run.\\
Seek to keep resigned and humble\\
Under His almighty hand,\\
Patient, willing, always ready\\
To fulfil His least command.
\end{stanza}
\begin{stanza}
Serving Him with joy and gladness,\\
Love pervading all your soul,\\
One in heart and mind and spirit,\\
Give Him absolute control.\\
He will mould and make you perfect,\\
Question not the Master's skill;\\
Silence all your thoughts and reasonings\\
In subjection to His will.
\end{stanza}
\begin{stanza}
Yield unmurmuring obedience,\\
Waver not nor turn aside:\\
When you waken with His likeness\\
You will then be satisfied.\\
Let Him now create unhindered,\\
Till His noble work is done,\\
And the Lord can see the image\\
Of His wellbelovèd Son.
\end{stanza}
\end{hymn}

\begin{hymn}{43}{The way that leads to heaven}{}
\begin{stanza}
The way that leads to heaven above\\
Is narrow, Jesus said.\\
Tis only those who truly love\\
God's way, therein will tread.
\end{stanza}
\begin{chorus}
\setchorusline{God's own way abides the same}
God's own way abides the same\\
In every age and clime.\\
There is but one true saving name:\\
The name of Christ divine.
\end{chorus}
\begin{stanza}
The Lord Himself has traced the way -\\
His feet were bruised and torn -\\
Exalted high in heaven today,\\
Where crowns His head adorn.
\end{stanza}
\begin{stanza}
That voice that called in accents clear,\\
The men of Galilee,\\
"Come, follow me, and do not fear,"\\
Is calling you and me.
\end{stanza}
\begin{stanza}
Vain creeds of men are sure to fail;\\
False theories pass away;\\
Alone shall truth for men avail\\
On that great judgment day.
\end{stanza}
\end{hymn}

\begin{hymn}{284}{I never can forget}{}
\begin{stanza}
I never can forget the day\\
The lowly Saviour came my way\\
And filled my soul with life divine;\\
The wealth of heaven now is mine.
\end{stanza}
\begin{chorus}
\setchorusline{I never can forget the day}
I never can forget the day\\
I heard the Saviour kindly say,\\
"If thou wouldst my disciple be,\\
Take up thy cross and follow me."
\end{chorus}
\begin{stanza}
I never can forget the love\\
That brought the Saviour from above,\\
Amid earth's multitudes to see\\
And set His loving eyes on me.
\end{stanza}
\begin{stanza}
He brought the heavens nigh to me,\\
The will of God so plain to see;\\
His simple truth, so sure and clear,\\
Was sweetest music to my ear.
\end{stanza}
\begin{stanza}
I never can forget the day\\
I learned to walk in Jesus' way;\\
Those feeble steps, with courage true,\\
Brought fellowship and life anew.
\end{stanza}
\end{hymn}

\begin{hymn}{62}{Sow the word}{}
\begin{stanza}
Sweet words of Jesus, eternal and true -\\
Like seeds of springtime, sow them anew;\\
Speak, speak the message that maketh me whole!\\
The words of Jesus breathe life to my soul.
\end{stanza}
\begin{chorus}
\setchorusline{Sow, sow the word, the Kingdom's seed!}
Sow, sow the word, the Kingdom's seed!\\
The words of Jesus are life, life indeed.
\end{chorus}
\begin{stanza}
Sow, then, O sower, in patience and love;\\
Precious the message sent from above.\\
I, so unworthy, so weak and defiled;\\
Speak, speak the gospel that makes me His child.
\end{stanza}
\begin{stanza}
Speak all the message, for I would be free;\\
All of His beauty cause me to see\\
That I may choose Him as life's better part,\\
That I may crown Him the king of my heart.
\end{stanza}
\end{hymn}

\begin{hymn}{26}{Die God van hemel}{\#403}
\begin{stanza}
Die God van hemel roep na my\\
Totdat my siel sy boodskap kry;\\
Al weet ek nie wat op my wag,\\
Sê ek so bly, ``Stuur my vandag.''
\end{stanza}
\begin{chorus}
\setchorusline{Gedring deur liefde gaan ek weer}
Gedring deur liefde gaan ek weer\\
Die sondaar te gaan soek, O Heer,\\
Want in my hart het daar ontvlam\\
n Brandend' liefde vir u Naam.
\end{chorus}
\begin{stanza}
Ek hoor die skape wat daar blêr\\
Ek sien die lammertjies van ver;\\
Ek luister na die sondaar pleit,\\
En sê, ``Stuur my, ek is bereid.''
\end{stanza}
\begin{stanza}
Ek durf nie vra, ``Heer stuur U my?''\\
Wyl duisende in sonde bly,\\
Maar alles in my hart roep uit,\\
Hier is ek Heer, ek sê dit luid.
\end{stanza}
\begin{stanza}
Sy kosbaar' Naam wil ek graag dra\\
In antwoord op sy groot gena'.\\
En meer soos Jesus wil ek wees\\
Volmaak patroon, dieselfde gees.
\end{stanza}
\end{hymn}

\begin{hymn}{42}{Dood in sonde, ver van God}{\#71}
\begin{stanza}
Dood in sonde, ver van God,\\
Soek die mens maar net genot:\\
Met geen God ontfermend goed;\\
Met geen hoop oor graf en dood.
\end{stanza}
\begin{chorus}
\setchorusline{God bied u sy saligheid –}
God bied u sy saligheid –\\
Dis nou u geleentheid.
\end{chorus}
\begin{stanza}
God roep u van sondeweë;\\
Roep u nou om toe te tree\\
Tot sy enge, ware weg,\\
Wat u voortlei in sy reg.
\end{stanza}
\begin{stanza}
Wil u sterf? Dit was vir u\\
Hy sy lewe het kom gee\\
Lewe wat sy lig laat skyn;\\
Bloed wat was van sonde rein.
\end{stanza}
\begin{stanza}
Die genadetyd gaan oor\\
As God u nie meer sal hoor;\\
Nou's die aangename tyd\\
Waar God nog so met u pleit.
\end{stanza}
\end{hymn}
